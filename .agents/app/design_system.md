# Design System & UI/UX Guidelines: YKIP Coffee-Themed Platform

## 1. Design Concept & Vibe
* **Theme:** "Secangkir Kopi untuk Pendidikan" (A Cup of Coffee for Education).
* **Atmosphere:** Warm, inviting, philanthropic, and premium. It should feel like sitting in a cozy, high-end cafe.
* **General Style:** Clean, spacious layouts with soft, rounded elements to avoid a rigid or "corporate" feel.

## 2. Color Palette
* **Primary Background (Cream/Beige):** Used for the main body background to give a warm, paper-like feel (approx. `#FDFBF7` or similar warm off-white).
* **Primary Accent (Coffee Brown):** Used for main headings, footer backgrounds, and primary buttons (approx. `#3E2723` or similar deep brown).
* **Secondary Accent (Caramel/Gold):** Used for the sub-footer, button gradients, and highlighting elements.
* **Surface Background (White):** Used for the floating cards (Order Form, Dynamic Impact Card, QRIS Receipt) to make them pop against the cream background.

## 3. Typography
* **Headings (Serif):** Elegant, traditional serif font for main titles (e.g., "Secangkir Kopi untuk Pendidikan", "Detail Pembayaran"). This establishes trust and a premium feel.
* **Body Text (Sans-Serif):** Clean, modern, highly readable sans-serif font for paragraphs, form labels, and button text.

## 4. UI Components & Elements
* **Buttons:** * Primary CTA buttons (like "Donate Now" or "Donasi") should be pill-shaped with full rounded edges.
    * Action buttons (like "Donasi Sekarang" with the receipt icon) can be rounded rectangles.
    * Use solid Coffee Brown or a Caramel-to-Cream gradient.
* **Cards & Containers:** * Soft rounded corners (approx. 16px - 24px border-radius).
    * Apply subtle, soft drop shadows to give a "floating" effect above the background.
* **Form Inputs:** * Minimalist approach. Instead of harsh, boxy borders, use clean lines (like a bottom border only) or very faint outlines to keep the aesthetic light.
* **Tier & Frequency Selectors:** * Tiers (50.000, 75.000, etc.) are styled as subtle square toggle buttons.
    * Frequencies (3 Bulan, 6 Bulan, etc.) are styled as pill-shaped toggle buttons.

## 5. Imagery & Assets
* **Background Textures:** The application uses subtle, hand-drawn/sketched illustrations of coffee beans and cups as a watermark/background layer.
* **Photography:** High-quality photos of children and education, framed with soft, warm borders.
* **Impact Vectors:** Clean, colorful vector graphics (e.g., the blue textbook) used exclusively inside the Dynamic Impact Card to contrast with the warm background and draw the eye.
* **QRIS Display:** The QRIS code is presented cleanly inside a structured "Receipt" style card with a decorative header, maintaining the professional payment standard while fitting the theme.
